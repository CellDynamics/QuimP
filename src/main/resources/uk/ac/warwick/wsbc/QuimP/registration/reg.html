Dear user

We are happy that you downloaded our software. Before you start using it we kindly ask you for registering it. This process takes less than minute and it is one time only. The key you will get is connected to email you provide. Therefore one key can be used across all yours QuimP installations.

Why we demand registration?

QuimP evolves as scientific software since 2002. Tracking of QuimP applications is important for us as scientists as well as for institutions that support its development.

"The Software Sustainability Institute cultivates world-class research with software. The Institute is based at the universities of Edinburgh, Manchester, Southampton and Oxford."

"We are one of seven Research Councils that work together as Research Councils UK (RCUK). We are funded by the Government's Department for Business, Innovation and Skills (BIS)."

How to register

  1. Follow the link pilip.lnx.warwick.ac.uk/registration.html.
  2. Eneter registration details on our web page.
  3. Check email for registration code.
  4. Enter the same details into this form.
On fresh installation you can use the same registration details.
